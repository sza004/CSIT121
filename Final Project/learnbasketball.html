Basketball Techniques and Basic Rules

Basic Rules:

  • Basketball is played between two teams, each aiming to score points by shooting the ball through the opponent's hoop.
  • Each team consists of five players on the court at a time.
  • Points are scored by making field goals worth two or three points, depending on the distance from the hoop, or free throws worth one point.
  • Players are not allowed to run while holding the ball (travelling), double dribble, or push other players.

Techniques:

Shooting:

Proper shooting technique involves positioning your body, focusing on the basket, and using a fluid motion to release the ball with proper spin and arc.

Learn more about shooting techniques here.

Dribbling:

Dribbling is the skill of bouncing the ball repeatedly with one hand while moving around the court. Keeping your head up while dribbling is crucial for awareness of the game.

Find more about dribbling techniques here.

Important Basketball Terms:

Term Definition
Assist Pass that leads directly to a score.
Rebound Act of retrieving the ball after a missed field goal or free throw.
